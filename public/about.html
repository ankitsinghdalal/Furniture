FURNITURETECH

We have exquisite passion and boundless curiosity to create a masterpiece for you!

Furniture Tech was established as a specialized privately owned furniture-manufacturing unit occupying 50,000 sq. ft. of state-of-the-art manufacturing equipment, highly skilled and specific workforce. We specialize in the designing and manufacture of high quality hospitality and commercial furniture. We also bring 25 years of experience in interior design, furniture and architecture with us. Furniture Tech functions on delivering the following values:

DESIGN & INNOVATION

Furniture Tech is passionate about developing fashionable and innovative home furnishings at a great value, as well as offering a number of exceptional services to meet your needs. It involves asking, looking, thinking, daydreaming, discussing, tinkering, and a lot of Brain Storming, failing, and trying again. It’s messy, but it works!

QUALITY & PERFORMANCE

Not a choice. Our products are manufactured to very high standards, using superior quality materials and craftsmanship with attention to detail. Furniture Tech has a fully implemented in-house quality management system and is committed to providing high quality products & service. We pride ourselves in our highly skilled workforce and combining them with the latest technology and techniques in woodwork, we strive to create various and high quality designs to suit your specific taste and style.

SERVICE

Furniture Tech’s products are backed by a comprehensive warranty providing you with peace of mind and an assurance of quality, reliability and performance. We take pride in having built good working relationships with our clients. We have done this by offering not only quality products, but also quality service. This is the reason that Furniture Tech is one of the most recognized brands in India for many of the leading corporate institutions, hotels and the government.

THE EXPERIENCE BEHIND THE REPUTATION

Over the years, Furniture Tech has worked with leading five-star hotels, multinational organizations, large corporate financial Institutions banks and the Government and is specified by leading Architects and Designers. We are constantly developing new lines and working to provide innovative furniture solutions.

Whether furnishing large or small corporate offices, training and conference facilities, boardrooms, hotels, lobby areas, restaurants or bar furniture, or be it home furniture in the form of bedroom, living room, kitchen, and outdoor furniture, Furniture Tech has the range and the quality to meet your needs. Our trained personnel can provide you with technical advice and suitability requirements on all products.
